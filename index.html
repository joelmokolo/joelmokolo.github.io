Primary Project

Operational Performance Intelligence Dashboard

SQL • Excel • Power BI • Data Modelling • Performance Analysis

A workforce and operational analytics project analysing performance, engagement, attendance and productivity across hybrid working teams.

Business Problem

Operational leaders need reliable insight into how working patterns affect productivity, engagement, absence and team performance.

Analysis Focus

  • Performance by working pattern
  • Engagement and productivity trends
  • Absence and workload indicators
  • Evidence-led operational decisions

Example SQL

SELECT e.work_mode,
       COUNT(DISTINCT e.employee_id) AS staff_count,
       AVG(p.calls_handled) AS avg_calls
FROM employees e
LEFT JOIN performance_metrics p
ON e.employee_id = p.employee_id
GROUP BY e.work_mode;

Key Insight

The project demonstrates how operational data can be structured, analysed and visualised to support workforce planning and service improvement.

Clinical Data Quality & Performance Analysis

SQL • Excel • Power BI • Data Quality • NHS-Style Reporting

An NHS-style analysis of appointment and patient pathway data, focused on attendance patterns, data quality issues and reporting reliability.

Business Problem

Missed appointments and poor-quality data can affect service planning, reporting accuracy and operational decision-making.

Analysis Focus

  • DNA rates by department
  • Waiting time impact on attendance
  • Missing, invalid and duplicate records
  • Validation before reporting

Example SQL

SELECT department,
       COUNT(patient_id) AS total_appointments,
       SUM(CASE WHEN attendance = 'DNA' THEN 1 ELSE 0 END) AS dna_count,
       (SUM(CASE WHEN attendance = 'DNA' THEN 1 ELSE 0 END) * 100.0)
       / COUNT(patient_id) AS dna_rate
FROM appointments
GROUP BY department;

Impact

Improving data quality and monitoring attendance patterns can support better scheduling, reduce missed appointments and improve service efficiency.

NHS Appointments DNA Analysis

Excel • Pivot Tables • KPI Development • Data Quality • Service Insight

An Excel-based analysis exploring outpatient missed appointment patterns and the relationship between waiting time and attendance.

Business Problem

Missed appointments can reduce clinical capacity, increase waiting times and affect patient pathway efficiency.

Analysis Focus

  • Total appointments
  • DNA count and DNA rate
  • DNA by department
  • DNA by waiting time group

KPI Logic

DNA Rate = (DNA Count / Total Appointments) * 100

Key Insight

Appointments booked more than 21 days ahead showed higher DNA rates, suggesting longer waits may reduce patient engagement.
